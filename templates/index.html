The electronic patient dossier

Health information, online available

In the electronic patient dossier (EPD), you as a (potential) patient can manage your health information and share it with (potential) doctors - if you want.

FAQ

Yes, the website and its use is not associated with any costs. We never ask for payment or credit cards!

The data is stored in a database to create the user account. We never share data with third parties.

First, you create a user account. There you can make various entries, e.g. a no-resuscitation declaration, your medications, previous illnesses, doctors and emergency contacts. If you opt for a shared account, you'll receive a public link to keep in your wallet.

If you opt for a shared account, you'll receive a public link to keep in your wallet. In the case of an emergency, a doctor can use this link and read your stored information. A doctor will not be able to change, delete or add your data. It`s only a readable version of your data.
